## 2. Solution 
- **Encoder**: BiLSTM → Linear projection to |L′| classes (incl. blank)
- **Forward–Backward (CTC)**: DP on the extended target `l′` with scaling `C_t, D_t`.
- **Gradients (Eq. 16)**:  via a autograd step.
- **Decoding**: Greedy best-path
